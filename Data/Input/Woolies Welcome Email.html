The really important stuff – bank details, tax and superannuation 
By now you would have received an email from our payroll system SuccessFactors with your login information. We may still be loading your details so if your access doesn’t work straight away, allow about 24 hours.
Click here for SuccessFactors
 
We want you to get taxed right so head to the ‘My Pay’ tile on the home page. Click on ‘My Tax Declaration’ and provide us with your right information.
 
For your Bank Account details, look for the ‘My Details and Job’ tile on the home page. Within your personal information you will find Payment Information.
You’ll want to select ‘Main Payment Method’ as the pay type and your BSB can be entered using format 000-000.
 
Under the Agreement that applies to you (as per our Offer Letter) you are entitled to choose which super fund you’d like your contributions to be paid to. You can make this choice by filling in a Super Choice Form [attached]. If you choose to complete this please email it to pspayroll@woolworths.com.au
If you don’t want to make a choice right now, your superannuation will be paid into the default fund under the Agreement which is the Retail Employees Superannuation Trust, or ‘REST’. If you don’t make a choice of fund, we’ll set up a REST Superannuation account for you, so no action is required from you (unless you want to change funds later, which you may).
Your REST account will be created when a contribution is paid. Membership information will be mailed to your home address.
